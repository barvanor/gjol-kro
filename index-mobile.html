Our story and vision

We - myself Peter and my fiancée Natazja leased the inn back in March 2020, with the vision of making the inn a sort of gathering place for all. A place where groups both large and small can enjoy a meal and also guests who just want to drink a cold beer in the bar area. It's not just for town locals, but also for guests from out of town.

Not just food but an experience
